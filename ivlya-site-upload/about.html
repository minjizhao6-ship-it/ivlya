About IVLYA

Packaging support for sellers who are ready to grow.

IVLYA is a one-stop e-commerce packaging solutions provider helping online businesses improve shipping, presentation, protection, and sourcing efficiency.

We connect packaging products with real fulfillment needs.

E-commerce packaging has to do more than look good. It must protect products, fit platform requirements, support fast packing, and represent the brand clearly when a customer opens the parcel.

  • Custom and stock packaging options for multiple product categories.
  • Practical advice for sellers working across Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, Etsy, and DTC channels.
  • Supply support for small batches, growing order volume, and warehouse replenishment.
