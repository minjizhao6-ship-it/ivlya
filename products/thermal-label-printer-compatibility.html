Selection criteria

Choose labels by printer structure, shipping workflow, and label environment.

For e-commerce sellers and 3PL warehouses, the right label is not only about size. Print clarity, adhesive stability, feeding format, and scanner readability all affect fulfillment speed.

Roll vs. Fanfold

Roll labels fit printers with internal media compartments or external holders. Fanfold labels are flat stacked labels, ideal for high-volume desktop shipping stations.

Core Size and Outer Diameter

Desktop printers commonly use a 1 inch core with smaller rolls. Industrial printers often use 3 inch cores and larger outer diameters for continuous warehouse printing.

Top-Coated Labels

Top-coated direct thermal labels help resist water, oil, and friction. They are useful for cross-border shipping, FBA prep, warehouse scanning, and long transit routes.
