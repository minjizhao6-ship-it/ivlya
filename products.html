Flexible Shipping Options for Global Buyers

We recommend shipping methods based on your experience, order size, and delivery requirements.

🚚

DDP (Recommended)

Door-to-door delivery with customs clearance and taxes included. Ideal for Shopify, Amazon, and first-time importers.

🚢

FOB

We handle production and export clearance; you manage freight from port. Best for experienced importers optimizing logistics cost.

🏭

EXW

Factory pickup with full buyer-controlled logistics. Best for buyers with their own freight forwarders.

✈️

Express Delivery

Fast courier service for samples and urgent replenishment orders.
